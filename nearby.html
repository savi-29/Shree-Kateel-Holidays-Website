Nearby Attractions

Explore the best places around Mangalore that showcase the beauty, culture and heritage of coastal Karnataka

Home › Near by Attraction
Krishna Matha

Famous for its Krishna Temple, unique temple architecture, vibrant religious activities.

Malpe Beach

One of the most popular beaches in Udupi, Malpe Beach is known for its golden sand, water sports activities, and the ferry service to St. Mary's Island.

Kateel Durga Parameshwari Temple

Located on an islet in the Nandini River, dedicated to Goddess Durga Parameshwari, scenic surroundings.

Kaup Beach

Famous for its historic lighthouse and scenic views, Kaup Beach is a great spot for photography and enjoying the coastal beauty.

Kadri Manjunath Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, stunning bronze statues, serene surroundings.

Delta Beach (Kodi Beach)

Known for its tranquility and the Kodi Bengre fishing village, it's a great place to experience local culture and enjoy fresh seafood.

Kudroli Gokarnath Temple

Modern temple dedicated to Lord Gokarnath, vibrant Navarathi celebrations, intricate carvings.

Panambur Beach

Known for its cleanliness and well-maintained surroundings, Panambur Beach is popular for water sports, kite festivals, and its picturesque sunsets.

Someshwara Beach

Famous for its golden sand and large rocks called "Rudra Shile," it's a less crowded beach perfect for a peaceful retreat.
